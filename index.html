About

A little Paris in Philly

Padmè is a Parisian style cafè located in the heart of Philladelphia. If you are looking for Philly's best coffee, or want to try awarding winning croissants, then make Padmè your final destination.

Menu

Organic and Sustainable

Everything on our menu is made in-house. All menu items are available for either eat-in or take-out. For large orders, please call 24hrs in advance.

Vegan items are designated with Ⓥ symbol.
